## The 3 copyright layers (every hymn has 3 parts)

Every hymn = **tune (melody) + text (lyrics) + arrangement (specific recording)**.

| Layer | Copyright expires when (US) |
|---|---|
| **Tune (melody)** | Composer died 70+ years ago, OR published 95+ years ago |
| **Text (lyrics)** | Author died 70+ years ago, OR published 95+ years ago |
| **Arrangement (recording)** | 95 years after publication in US (pre-1924 recordings are public domain as of 2025) |

**All three must be PD** (or you have a license) to freely distribute a recording.

## US public-domain day timeline

| Year works entered PD | Today (2026) |
|---|---|
| 1928 works | PD since Jan 2024 |
| 1929 works + 1924 sound recordings | PD since Jan 2025 |
| **1930 works** | **PD since Jan 2026** |
| 2030 | All pre-1935 hymns fully PD |

**Practical rule:** any hymn written **before 1930** is in the US public domain today (text + tune). Specific recordings made before 1924 are also PD (sound recordings).

## What we will NOT do

❌ **No scraping of Spotify / YouTube / Apple Music.** Those platforms have license terms; pulling from them = infringement.
❌ **No use of modern worship recordings without a license.** Hillsong, Bethel, Sovereign Grace, Shane & Shane, etc. are all copyrighted.
❌ **No "AI-cover" generated vocals for copyrighted hymns.** Even with new AI vocals, the tune + text may still be copyrighted.

## What we WILL do (the production path)

✅ **Download from hymnstogod.org** for the 21 PD hymns (CC0/PD license)
✅ **Tag every download in PB** with:
- `audio_license`: "Public Domain" or "CC0"
- `audio_source`: "hymnstogod.org" or specific attribution
- `source_url`: the direct download URL
- `recording_year`: per the source
✅ **Skip the 5 copyrighted hymns** (As the Deer, Good Good Father, Tremble, In Christ Alone, 10,000 Reasons) — leave them as Path C instrumental-only OR remove
✅ **Add player UI attribution** for every song played ("Recorded by [artist name] via hymnstogod.org, public domain")
